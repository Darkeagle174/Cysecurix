Defensive

Defensive-level cybersecurity jobs focus on protecting an organization's systems and data from cyber threats. Roles such as Security Analyst, Incident Responder, and Blue Team Member involve monitoring networks, analyzing security breaches, and implementing measures to prevent attacks. These professionals use advanced tools and techniques to detect vulnerabilities, respond to incidents, and fortify defenses. Their work is crucial in maintaining the integrity, confidentiality, and availability of sensitive information, ensuring robust protection against ever-evolving cyber threats.
